рџ’Ў Project Motivation

As the Class Representative (CR) of my class, I had to calculate the GPA and CGPA of many students manually after every semester result. This process was time-consuming and prone to calculation errors.

To solve this real-world problem, I developed the Student Academic Portal, which automates GPA and CGPA calculations for our course. Students can simply enter their grades, and the application instantly calculates their GPA and CGPA, tracks arrears, predicts future CGPA, and securely stores academic records locally. This significantly reduces manual effort and makes academic record management easier.
